# Vignette 019 – Close

> Alis was standing with Vic and Solana in front of the interdimensional portal; the night was drawing to a close.
>
> With a gentle wave of her hand, Solana says, "It was nice meeting you, Alis!"
>
> Alis lightly dips his head. "Likewise," he replies.
>
> "Take care of yourself back in your universe," Vic tells him.
>
> "Of course."

---

> With a droll smile, Alis adds, ["You didn't bring your exotic dessert this time."](../2022/2022-11-05_vignette-004_enthralled.md)
>
> Vic blinks. A moment later, he sputters, "Aha, you didn't notice?"
>
> "Notice...?" Alis implores, eyebrows raised.
>
> "The astringent in your ice cream."
>
> "...You're claiming that's derived from larvae and not berries," Alis flatly says.
>
> Vic stiffly nods.
>
> Alis stares dryly at Vic, then scoffs with a faint smile.

---

> "...Before I leave, I should mention. In my universe, scarves are traditionally used as a symbol of engagement." He gestures to Vic. "Yours suits you quite well."
>
> "Is that right?" Vic says. "That's rather interesting!"
>
> A moment later, he tilts his head. "Or... is that another fabrication of yours?"
>
> Alis shrugs. Stepping through to the other side of the portal, he adds, "You tell me, my dear ice cream connoisseur."

---

> Vic grumbles to himself, then says, "Oh, fine. The ice cream thing was a gag. It's just mixed berries and a touch of honey. There, I gave a straight answer—now will you—"
>
> "—Goodbye," Alis interjects.
>
> With that, Alis's irises turn gold, and Vic's follow suit. Vic presses a few controls on the interdimensional portal, and the veil of the portal vanishes—and along with that, Alis.
>
> Immediately afterward, Vic's eyes return to their normal color. After regaining awareness, Vic sighs with exasperation and smiles.
>
> "...Of course."
