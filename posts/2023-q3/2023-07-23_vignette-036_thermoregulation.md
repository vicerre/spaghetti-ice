# Vignette 036 – Thermoregulation

> Things had changed since she last set foot into Vic's bedroom; Clementine had sprawled out onto the bed, and Solana had migrated to the ground at the bed's feet, where she was now sitting with her legs splayed, laptop in hand.
>
> "Hmm, curious."
>
> Solana looks up.
>
> Vic had just joined her on the ground, sitting adjacent to her.
>
> "I can't seem to bring myself to adopt my public persona," he says.
>
> Solana folds the laptop and looks curiously at him.
>
> "Oh huh," she says. "Is it because of me, this room," she asks, pointing, "or your lack of shoes?"
>
> Vic chuckles a little, then replies "yes".

---

> "You requested a visit to my residence for the express purpose of thermoregulation," Vic states.
>
> He lifts his eyebrows in droll expectation. "Something tells me you aren't referring to the aging cooling system in this place."
>
> Solana snickers. "I mean," she says, "you have ice powers, so it's only natural you'll get asked, right?"
>
> "It's clearly my destiny to be someone's personal cooler," he replies flatly, lightly smirking.
>
> With a shrug, she replies, "I mean, you have to admit you're cool in more ways than one."
>
> He chuckles. "Touché."

---

> Solana thinks for a moment, then pokes his nose.
>
> Looking at him with a soft, curious expression, she asks, "Touché?"
>
> Vic looks at her warmly, then scoffs. "Touché indeed."
>
> Solana slowly leans towards him, then presses her body against his shoulder. As she does, a sprinkling of icicles precipitates beside Vic's feet, jittering briefly before dissipating.
>
> For a moment, Vic looked as though he had something to say.
>
> Instead, he reaches for her hand, clasping it with his own.
